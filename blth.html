📶 How Bluetooth and Wi-Fi Work

📖 Introduction

Bluetooth and Wi-Fi are wireless technologies that connect devices without cables.

📡 What is Wi-Fi?

Wi-Fi uses radio waves to provide internet access.

How it Works

  • Router connects to internet
  • Router sends signals
  • Device receives signals
  • Data is transferred

🔵 What is Bluetooth?

Bluetooth is used for short-distance communication.

How it Works

  • Devices pair
  • Secure connection is formed
  • Data is transferred

🔍 Differences

Feature Wi-Fi Bluetooth
Range Long Short
Speed High Low
Power Usage High Low

🌐 Examples

Wi-Fi for internet, Bluetooth for earphones and smart devices.

🎯 Conclusion

Both technologies are important for modern wireless communication.
