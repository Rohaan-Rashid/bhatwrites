Top 10 Science Facts That Will Blow Your Mind

Amazing Facts You Probably Didn’t Know

1. Your Brain Uses a Lot of Energy

Your brain uses about 20% of your body’s total energy, even when you are resting.

2. Bananas Are Slightly Radioactive

Bananas contain potassium, which has a small amount of natural radioactivity.

3. Water Can Boil and Freeze at the Same Time

This happens at a special condition called the triple point, where solid, liquid, and gas exist together.

4. Octopuses Have Three Hearts

Two hearts pump blood to the gills, and one pumps it to the rest of the body.

5. The Sun Makes Up Most of the Solar System’s Mass

About 99.8% of the mass of the solar system is in the Sun.

6. You Can’t Taste Food Without Saliva

Saliva helps dissolve food so taste receptors can detect flavors.

7. Lightning Is Hotter Than the Sun’s Surface

A lightning bolt can be several times hotter than the surface of the Sun.

8. Sharks Are Older Than Trees

Sharks existed about 400 million years ago, before trees appeared on Earth.

9. The Human Body Glows (But We Can’t See It)

The human body emits a small amount of light due to chemical reactions, but it is too weak to be seen by the human eye.

10. Space Is Completely Silent

There is no air in space, so sound cannot travel.

Conclusion

Science is full of amazing facts that can change how we see the world. Learning these facts not only increases knowledge but also makes science more fun and interesting.
