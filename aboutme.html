About Us

Welcome to Bhat Writes!
My name is Rohaan Rashid, and I am a student passionate about learning, technology, and sharing knowledge.

Who We Are?

This website is created to provide useful and easy-to-understand information on different topics such as technology, cricket, health, and programming. Our goal is to make learning simple and accessible for everyone.

Our Mission:

At Bhat Writes, our mission is to help students and learners understand concepts clearly and improve their knowledge step by step. We aim to provide high-quality content that is simple, engaging, informative, and easy to understand. Today, many people spend hours endlessly scrolling on their mobile phones because they quickly lose interest in what they read online. We want to change that. Our goal is to create content across different categories so that if a reader does not find interest in one topic, they can easily explore another section of our website and discover something meaningful and engaging. We believe reading should feel interesting, valuable, and enjoyable — not boring or complicated. Our mission is simple: To make people read, learn, and explore instead of endlessly scrolling.
